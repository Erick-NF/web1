"Methodologies Traditional"
They are those that establish a work discipline on the software development process, with the purpose of achieving a more efficient software




"Methodologies Agils"
It is a form of project management, which has been used mainly in software development and which proposes that the solutions offered are defined as a team and in collaboration with customers
